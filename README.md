## What is NexaIQ?

NexaIQ is a production-grade B2B SaaS platform that transforms raw business data into autonomous AI-driven decisions. Upload a CSV, and the platform automatically ingests, transforms, trains ML models, detects anomalies, and delivers executive alerts — no data team required.
